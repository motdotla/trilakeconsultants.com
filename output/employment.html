Employment

Tri Lake Consultants regularly has positions available for talented individuals with experience in the civil engineering and public works construction fields. We are particularly interested in qualified applicants for the following positions:

  • Public Works Inspector
  • Civil Engineer
  • Civil Designer/CAD Technician

If you would like to be considered as openings are available, please submit your resume to our office via fax. Past experience with municipalities and an understanding of capital improvement project construction is preferred.
